FSNAP32K.IMG

Snap-Display:   480 * 400 at 32k colors
Snap-Date:   15 Jan 1996
Snap-Software:   Snapper
Conversion-Software:   imgtools

This is an authentic test snapshot when TIMG (TrueColor IMG extension) support became available in Snapper and 1stGuide.

Creating an appropriate PNG equivalent forced development of the img2png portable software utility (more than 256 colors, so no alternative conversion process preserving the color depth was available).
